CHIX ON THE GO

Chix on the Go by Three - Y is a newly established food delivery service based in Malibay, Pasay City. The owners, Meng and Imee Rosel, started their business on May 8, 2020. They currently don’t have a physical store and are accepting orders through their Facebook account. They offer a variety of homemade dishes such as different kinds of pancit, flavored bangus, lumpiang shanghai, siomai, chicken sisig, and their specialty, flavored fried chicken.
